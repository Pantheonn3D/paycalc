Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about salary calculations and taxes

How accurate is this calculator?

Our calculator uses the latest tax rates and withholding formulas to provide estimates that are typically within 1-2% of actual paycheck amounts. However, individual circumstances can vary, especially with complex tax situations. For personalized tax advice, please consult a tax professional.

Does this calculator consider all possible deductions?

The calculator includes common deductions like retirement contributions, health insurance, and standard tax deductions. For specialized deductions, use the "Other Deductions" fields.

How are state taxes calculated?

State tax calculations use the latest published tax rates and brackets for each state. We account for state-specific deductions and exemptions where applicable. States with no income tax (like Texas, Florida, and others) will show $0 for state tax.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. All calculations are performed in your browser, and we don't store any of your personal or financial information on our servers.
